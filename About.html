Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson is a legendary WWE wrestler, actor, and producer, widely regarded as one of the greatest professional wrestlers in history. A third-generation superstar, he became a defining icon of WWE’s "Attitude Era" (1996-2004) and returned as a part-time superstar and "Final Boss," later joining the TKO board of directors.
